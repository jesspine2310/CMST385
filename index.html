About Alanis Morissette

Alanis Morissette is a groundbreaking artist known for her innovative sound, relateable lyrics, and captivating performances.

 

She was born on June 1, 1974 at Roverside Hospital in Ottawa Ontario. Her family consists of her parents, Georgia and Alan, and her two brothers, Chad and her twin brother Wade.

 

At age 6 years old, she started to play the piano, and composed her first song at 10!

 

Alanis would soon later release her demo "Fate Stay With Me" in 1989; and shortly after, released her debut album, Alanis, in 1991.
